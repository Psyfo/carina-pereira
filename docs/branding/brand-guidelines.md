# Carina Pereira International - Brand Identity Guide

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** Carina Pereira International  
**Tagline:** "Make-up made easy with Carina"  
**Industry:** Beauty Education & Professional Makeup Artistry  
**Established:** Professional makeup academy and services provider

## Mission Statement

To democratize professional makeup education by providing accessible, high-quality training that transforms passion into profitable careers. We bridge the gap between amateur enthusiasm and professional expertise through internationally recognized certification programs.

## Brand Values

- **Excellence**: Industry-leading education with MAC Cosmetics heritage
- **Accessibility**: Flexible learning options (full-time, part-time, online)
- **Empowerment**: Building confidence through practical, hands-on training
- **Inclusivity**: Welcoming students of all skill levels and backgrounds
- **Authenticity**: Real-world experience with brand shoots and weddings
- **Innovation**: Cutting-edge techniques with premium tools and products

## Target Audience

### Primary Segments

1. **Aspiring Makeup Artists** (18-35)

   - Career changers seeking professional certification
   - Beauty enthusiasts wanting to monetize their passion
   - Students seeking internationally recognized qualifications

2. **Busy Professionals** (25-45)

   - Individuals wanting flexible online learning
   - Part-time students balancing work and education
   - Those seeking personal skill development

3. **Beauty Service Seekers** (20-50)
   - Brides and bridal parties
   - Special occasion makeup clients
   - Photography and editorial clients

```css
Primary Colors:

- cpCream: Background, neutral spaces
```

### Typography

```css
Primary Font: 'Tan Ashford' (Display/Headers)
- Usage: Main headings, brand name, course titles
- Style: Bold, distinctive, luxury feel
- Weight: Bold (normal)

Secondary Font: 'Inclusive Sans' (Body Text)
- Usage: Body text, descriptions, general content
- Style: Clean, readable, accessible
- Weights: Regular (400), Italic

Accent Font: 'Inter' (Supporting Text)
- Usage: Technical text, captions, metadata
- Style: Modern, clean, professional
- Weights: Regular (400), Italic
```

## Brand Standards

### Do's

- Use lowercase styling for headings ("pro make-up course")
- Maintain consistent color usage across platforms
- Emphasize practical, hands-on learning benefits
- Highlight international recognition and MAC heritage
- Use authentic, professional imagery

### Don'ts

- Compromise on visual quality or professionalism
- Use inconsistent typography or color schemes
- Make false claims about outcomes or certifications
- Neglect mobile responsiveness
- Use stock imagery that doesn't align with brand aesthetic
